\section{Problem 4}

Let $\{z^k\}_{k\ge0}$ be the iterates generated by the GPM,
and the update rule is,
\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
    w^k &\leftarrow z^k+\frac{\alpha}{n}Cz^k, \\
    z^{k+1} &\leftarrow \frac{w^k}{|w^k|}.
\end{split}
\end{equation}
So
\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
    f(z^{k+1})-f(z^k)
    &= (z^{k+1})^HCz^{k+1} - (z^k)^HCz^k \\
    &= |(z^{k+1})^Hz^*|^2 - |(z^k)^Hz^*|^2 +
        (z^{k+1})^H\Delta z^{k+1} - (z^k)^H\Delta z^k \\
    &= (z^{k+1})^H\Delta z^{k+1} - (z^k)^H\Delta z^k \\
\end{split}
\end{equation}
