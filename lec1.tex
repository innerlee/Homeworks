\subsection{} %1.1

$(M^{2n}, \omega)$, $M$ closed, $\omega$ \emph{symplectic}.

\begin{framed}
    A symplectic form is a $2$-form satisfying an algebraic condition --
    nondegeneracy -- and an analytical condition -- closedness.

    $d\omega=0$, $\omega^n$ is a volume form.
\end{framed}
